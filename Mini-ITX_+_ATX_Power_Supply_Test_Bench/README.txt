# Summary

This is a simple test bench fitting a Mini-ITX (17 x 17cm) motherboard and an ATX power supply in vertical position.

# Print Settings

Printer Brand: Prusa
Printer: i3 MK3
Rafts: No
Supports: No
Resolution: 0.2
Infill: 10%
Filament_brand: Das Filament
Filament_color: Green
Filament_material: PLA or PETG

Notes: 
Please check your power supply dimensions before printing, especially the position of the 2 screws holding the back.

The "AB" STL file is there for showing the assembled parts, not for printing in one piece.

# Post-Printing

## Requirements

- 4x brass motherboard standoffs and M3 screws to hold the motherboard
- 5x M3 screws (3x front, 2x back) to hold the power supply in place
- 2x M4x30 mm screws and nuts (8mm wide) to hold the "B" piece vertically from the bottom "A" plate

The screw holes are not threaded, use one screw to do it before assembling.

## Possible improvements

For a next version of the design :
- better holders for the 2 M4 nuts, they can move quite easily when screwing
- or get rid the the M4 screws altogether
